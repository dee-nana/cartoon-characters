Little Mermaid

"The Little Mermaid" follows the story of Ariel, a curious and adventurous mermaid princess who longs to explore the human world above the sea. Disobeying her father's warnings, she strikes a deal with the sea witch Ursula to trade her voice for a chance to be human and be with Prince Eric, whom she rescued and fell in love with after a shipwreck.

Transformed into a human, Ariel faces challenges in adapting to her new form and navigating the complexities of human life. With the help of her friends, including a colorful crab named Sebastian, she strives to win Eric's heart and break Ursula's curse. However, Ursula's manipulations lead to a climactic battle where Ariel's love and determination are tested.

"The Little Mermaid" is a tale of self-discovery, sacrifice, and the pursuit of true love.
